Notes

JP1 - syncronization input e.g. from push-pull
JP18 - sync. edge selection
JP2 - output polarity (driver inversion/without inversion)
RV2 - burst delay
RV3 - burst duration
JP3 - sync. out (if needed)
JP17 - sync. polarity
RV1 - HF frequency
RV4 - HF duty factor
J1 - power supply 12-24v
J2 - to inductor
J3 - HV PSU (50-200v)
D5,6 - 16v zeners (or similar)
D7 - HER860 or similar
D8 - some suitable snabber diode

C2,C5,C8,C14 are optional, install only if you need adjust timing
